Our Story

Introducing Como Baltic

Since 2006, We've built baltic tubes for steel infrastructure experts

We are a joint-stock company Baltic Tube Service that specializes in supplying new and second-hand large steel pipes and steel sheet piles.

With experience across European projects, We do port development, pipeline construction, and trenchless method operations.

We operate on projects of varying complexity and scale, offering tailored solutions to meet client needs. Our team ensures fast delivery of steel pipes from our warehouse to any location in Europe, prioritizing efficiency and mutual benefit.
